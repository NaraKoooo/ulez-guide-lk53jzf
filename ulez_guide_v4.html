1 What is the Problem? 問題是什麼？

Riding in London, your motorcycle may need to pay a £12.50 ULEZ daily charge because ULEZ requires motorcycles to have NOx emissions ≤ 0.15 g/km. Your 2003 Piaggio 124cc is a Euro 2 vehicle, which has a NOx limit of 0.3 g/km — theoretically above the ULEZ threshold.

在倫敦騎車，你的車可能需要每天繳 £12.50 ULEZ 費用，因為 ULEZ 要求摩托車的 NOx 排放量必須 ≤ 0.15 g/km。你的車是 2003年 Piaggio 124cc，屬於 Euro 2 標準，Euro 2 的 NOx 上限是 0.3 g/km，理論上超標。

💡 Don't worry! 0.3 g/km is the maximum allowed for Euro 2 — not your bike's actual emission. Just like a 70mph speed limit doesn't mean you always drive at 70mph. Your bike's real NOx could be as low as 0.05–0.12 g/km, well within the ULEZ limit!

💡 但別擔心！ 0.3 g/km 是 Euro 2 的「最高允許值」，不是你的車實際排放的數字。就像速限 70mph，不代表你一定開 70mph。你的車實際 NOx 可能只有 0.05–0.12 g/km，完全符合標準！

2 What is NOx? NOx 是什麼？

NOx = Nitrogen Oxides, a toxic gas produced when an engine burns fuel at high temperatures. The hotter and dirtier the engine, the more NOx it produces.

NOx = 氮氧化物，是引擎燃燒汽油時排出的有毒廢氣。溫度越高、引擎越髒，NOx 就越多。

How it is produced:

燃燒過程：

⛽ Fuel + 💨 Air (nitrogen + oxygen) ⛽ 汽油 + 💨 空氣（氮 + 氧）
↓
🔥 High-temperature combustion in engine 🔥 引擎高溫燃燒
↓
💧 Normal exhaust gases (CO₂ + H₂O) 💧 正常廢氣（CO₂ + H₂O）
↓
☠️ NOx — toxic by-product; more heat = more NOx ☠️ NOx — 有毒副產品，溫度越高越多

Factors that affect NOx levels:

影響 NOx 多少的因素：

Factor因素 NOx Increases ↑NOx 增加 ↑ NOx Decreases ↓NOx 減少 ↓
Combustion temperature燃燒溫度 Higher temperature溫度越高 Lower temperature溫度越低
Air-fuel mixture空燃比 Too much air (lean mixture)空氣太多（稀薄混合） Correct mixture ratio油氣比例正確
Engine condition引擎狀態 Carbon build-up, worn spark plug積碳、老化、火星塞舊 Clean, well-maintained乾淨、保養好
Carburettor化油器 Poorly tuned調校失準 Correctly tuned調校正確
