🔧 Vespa Repair Checklist

2003 Piaggio 124cc — Maintenance & Repair Priority List

LK53 JZE
📸 Current Condition Photos
🔴 Urgent — Do First 3 items
  • 1
    ⚙️ Engine Tune — Spark Plug + Oil + Carburettor
    Engine wear / carbon build-up / carburettor out of tune → NOx rises (possibly 0.20–0.25 g/km). Tuning before the ULEZ emissions test significantly improves pass rate.
    引擎老化 / 積碳 / 化油器失調 → NOx 升高（可能 0.20–0.25 g/km）
    ↓ 做保養
    ① 換火星塞（約 £5–15，自己換）
    ② 換機油（約 £20–30，自己換）
    ③ 調整化油器（約 £30–50，找技師）— 最重要！
    ↓ 調整後
    NOx 降回正常水平（估計 0.05–0.12 g/km）✅
    ① £5–15 DIY ② £20–30 DIY ③ £30–50 Mechanic
  • 2
    ⛽ Fuel Gauge Indicator — Replace
    The fuel gauge is not working correctly. Needs replacement to avoid running out of fuel unexpectedly. 油表指示壞掉，需要更換，避免不知道油量。
    Est. £15–40 Mechanic
  • 3
    💡 Front Left Indicator Light — Replace
    The front left indicator light needs replacing. A non-functioning indicator is an MOT failure point and a road safety issue. 前面左邊燈照要換，指示燈壞掉會導致 MOT 不過，也是行車安全問題。
    Est. £5–20 DIY possible
⚪ Not Urgent — When Budget Allows 4 items
  • 1
    🪑 Seat / Saddle — Replace
    Current seat is cracked and worn (visible tape repair). Cosmetic issue but worth replacing for comfort and appearance. 椅墊已破裂（可見膠帶修補），影響外觀和舒適度，不急但建議更換。
    Est. £30–80 DIY possible
  • 2
    🛡️ Footboard / Lower Body Panel — Replace
    The lower body shell / footboard area has damage and wear. Cosmetic repair, not structurally critical. 腳下外殼有損壞，純外觀問題，不影響行車安全。
    Est. £20–60 DIY possible
  • 3
    🔩 Exhaust Pipe — Replace (Rusted)
    The exhaust pipe is visibly rusted. While not immediately dangerous, a severely rusted exhaust can affect performance and eventually fail MOT. 排氣管生鏽，目前不緊急，但嚴重生鏽會影響性能並可能導致 MOT 不過。
    Est. £40–120 Mechanic recommended
  • 4
    🐝 Vespa Logo / Badge — Replace
    The Vespa logo badge is missing or damaged. Pure cosmetic — makes the bike look more complete. Vespa 標誌缺失或損壞，純外觀，讓車看起來更完整。
    Est. £5–15 DIY
