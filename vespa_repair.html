    ⚙️ Engine Tune — Spark Plug + Oil + Carburettor 引擎調整 — 換火星塞 + 換機油 + 調整化油器
    Engine wear / carbon build-up / carburettor out of tune → NOx rises (possibly 0.20–0.25 g/km). Tuning before the ULEZ emissions test significantly improves pass rate. 引擎老化 / 積碳 / 化油器失調 → NOx 升高，在 ULEZ 排放測試前做保養可大幅提高通過率。

    ↓ Service the bike ↓ 做保養
